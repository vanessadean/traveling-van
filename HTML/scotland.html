Scotland


Scotland

I've been to Scotland a few times and this last trip was a quick one to Loch Lomond, near Glasgow, for a wedding. Scottish weddings weren't something on my radar before this trip, but now I know better than to pass up an opportunity to party with the Scots. There were tons of good food and booze, a band playing traditional Scottish music and a little something called Ceilidh dancing. It felt a lot wilder in person, but here is a taste.



The wedding and reception were held in a beautiful old baronial manor house turned youth hostel, where everyone was booked in for the weekend. The hostel has since closed, which is a shame because it was a fantastic place to host a party.

After the wedding we spent just one night in Glasgow before heading back to the States. We stayed in a trendy-looking little hotel in the city center called The Brunswick.

The best surprise for me though was the tasty food at Dakhin, a South Indian restaurant right around the corner from the hotel. I absolutely stuffed myself on dosas. It was a great ending to a fantastic week and a half of traveling and festivities.
